Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Adst Stock Education (https://adst.blog) uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience and analyze website traffic.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit websites. They help us understand how users interact with our content and improve site functionality.

How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function correctly and cannot be switched off.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use Google Analytics to gather data on site usage to improve performance.
  • Preference Cookies: These remember your settings and preferences for future visits.

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be placed by third-party services, such as embedded videos or analytics tools.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Please check this page periodically for any changes.
